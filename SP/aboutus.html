about us

Sehatpal is a digital healthcare platform developed by a Karachi-based team, offering AI-powered symptom checks, real-time consultations with licensed professionals, and secure medical-record management.
Created by undergraduate students Syed Ukkashah (Backend Developer) and Ibrahim Johar (Front‑End Developer).
Our platform delivers seamless access to healthcare tools alongside expert-curated educational resources developed in collaboration with doctors, psychologists, and wellness coaches.
We prioritize user feedback and partner with local clinics to tailor services to diverse cultural and medical needs.
At Sehatpal, compassion, innovation, and reliability converge to ensure quality healthcare is accessible to everyone.
