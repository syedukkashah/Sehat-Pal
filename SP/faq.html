Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. All data is stored securely in compliance with healthcare privacy regulations.

How do I access my medical records?

After logging in, navigate to the "Medical Records" section. You can view, download, or share your records with healthcare providers.

Can I update my medical information?

Yes, you can update your medical information at any time through your account dashboard. All changes are logged for security purposes.

How does the chatbot work?

Our AI-powered chatbot can answer general health questions, provide information about medical conditions, and help you find relevant healthcare resources.

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yes, the chatbot is available 24/7 to provide immediate assistance with your health-related queries.

How is my data protected?

We implement multiple layers of security including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your data.
